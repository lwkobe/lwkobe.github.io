Biography

Wei Li is now a Ph.D. student at FLARE LAB advised by Prof. Xiaopei Liu. Wei received his B.S. degree in computer science from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Shaanxi, China in 2015.

He is interested in making virtual worlds with computer, and more specifically in the area of computer graphics, physically-based animation, computational physics, scientific computing, rendering, and artificial intelligence.
