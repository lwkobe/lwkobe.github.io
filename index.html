About me

I am a PhD candidate in the School of Information Science & Technology of ShanghaiTech University in China, supervised by Prof. Xiaopei Liu , and majoring in computer graphics, with a particular focus on fluid simulations.

Differing from traditional fluid simulation, my research is multi-disciplinary, covering many aspects in computer science and computational physics, as well as some artificial intelligence. During my PhD study, I have closely collaborated with Prof. Mathieu Desbrun from Caltech and Prof. Changxi Zheng from Columbia University for fluid simulation.

I received my BS degree in computer science from Northwestern Polytechnical University, China, supervised by Prof. Yong Xia in 2015.
